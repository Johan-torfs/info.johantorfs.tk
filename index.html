Background

Early interests

Since early childhood I have been fascinated by computers. I must admit that at this point mostly by computer games, but that is still what has sparked my interest in making my own games and programs. The biggest turning point for me was the invention of Minecraft. Like many kids from my generation, I played Minecraft day in and day out, but this was also the first time I came into contact with programming. Trying to write mods for this game, made me fall in love with making my own creations through code.

Programming also came with graphics design, but for a long time both of these stayed a hobby. Some of my studies would have one or two aspects related to programming, but most of my time at this point would be spend on other things. And so my programming journey came to a hold.

Studies

After I finished high school, I went to university. I spend several years here, but in the end I was unable to accomplish anything here. So I went to pursue my other interest, teaching. From past experiences, I knew that I enjoyed helping people learn new things and I also enjoyed working with kids. Regretfully I found that I also wasn't cut out for teaching in high school Having tried my best at studying, I decided it was time to start working. Even without a degree, I would rather start working than feeling stuck not being able to finish an education.

Present & Future

At the moment I am working for 't Ballonneke, a day-care centre in Beveren. As my employer noticed that I possessed a different skillset than most of my colleagues, I was asked to assist in the digitalisation of our current working environment. One example of this is a kind of lending service we provide to out different locations. With this lending service the different locations have access to a common stock of toys, which can circulate through different locations. Right now this is a lot of paperwork and the system works quite counterintuitive. This led to me reworking this system into a web shop where colleagues can decide which toys they would like to use on their location for a few months and where a few admins will be able to regulate this process.

In the future I hope to be able to work on a lot of different IT-related projects. Whether it be webdesign, graphics design or other computer related tasks, by this point it is clear to me that I have a passion for this kind of work and would like nothing more than to find and complete challenging IT-related projects.
