Using colors correctly

Choosing a good color scheme for your website is not easy if you’re not a skilled designer. Visit sites with a focus on the same type of visitors to study the design. This is what color psychology brings to you:

  • Cheap website

    Combine blue/yellow/red in a positive design = white background with black text
    https://www.zeeman.com/

  • Luxurious products

    Combine shades of grey/metallic tints in a positive design
    https://www.tesla.com/nl_be/

  • A website for a wide audience

    Light blue is okay for everyone, use it in a positive design
    https://www.wikipedia.org/

  • A website for a young audience, mainly men

    Negative design = black background and white text
    https://www.callofduty.com/be/nl/

  • A website visited by women

    All stereotypes are true, pink and purple is okay
    https://www.flair.be/nl/

  • A website for kids

    Choose a very colorful design
    https://www.ketnet.be/

The colorwheel

Colors evoke emotions, moods, and feelings. Each designer has to know the power of color psychology. It helps you make better design decisions. A good color scheme consists of multiple colors. Besides white and black you should choose at least 1 extra color with a matching accent color for small decorations. More information on matching colors based on the color wheel. If you really don’t know how to match colors, try to get inspired by nature. Colors we see often together in nature, match quite well.

Adobe helps you: https://color.adobe.com

Creating space

Whether you are creating a website for a general public or for a specific target audience, you always want to make sure your website has enough blank space. When we are looking at a webpage that is too bloated with information, we won't be able to filter the imporant information. We won't be able to focus on specific parts of the site and our gaze will drift off, making it even more difficult to focus. But most importantly if we don't leave enough space on a website our eyes won't have space to rest and will tire out very quickly.
